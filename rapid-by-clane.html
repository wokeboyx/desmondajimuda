The Revolutionary POS Solution for Merchants

DESIGN FOR MOBILE
Overview

Rapid is the second product in Clane's two-sided network, with Clane catering to consumers and Rapid catering to merchants. Rapid was designed to replace the conventional POS device by facilitating sales collection for merchants and agents through various payment methods, including bank transfers, USSD, Bluetooth payment, Clane transfer, and QR payment. This project was the primary focus for Clane in 2021, as the consumer aspect of their two-sided network (Clane app) was already available in the market.

My role

Led and oversaw the design of this mobile app.

THe team

2 designers, 1 product manager 3 engineers.

Timeline

October 2021 - March 2022

the problem

Small and large business merchants and agents in Nigeria are looking for simple and affordable tools to start, grow and run their business, there is a lot of mechanical burden in the process of receiving money as the POS device which has become an obligatory piece of merchant equipment are expensive, quickly become outdated, and take a significant portion of the merchantâ€™s revenue.

Design solution

We build an innovative solution that will replace the traditional POS for merchants. It caters to the needs of merchants who want to collect sales, perform agency banking, or both, by transforming their phone into a POS. This solution saves the cost of maintaining or purchasing a POS and promotes contactless payment.

Conclusion

To gauge the market's response to this innovation, we conducted a soft launch of our product with a select group of merchants. We received real-time feedback and insights, indicating that the MVP was well-received by the merchants. However, there were areas that required improvement. Our research revealed that enterprise merchants desired more than just the ability to collect sales and run agency banking. They also sought a business management tool.Based on this feedback, we enhanced the product's functionality before rebranding Clane's two-sided network. The rebranding exercise involved renaming Clane's customer side to ClanePay and Rapid to ClanePOS.
