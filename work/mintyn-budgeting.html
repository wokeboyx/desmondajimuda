Mintyn Budgeting

2023
Project Overview
Mintyn is a digital bank app that aims to provide users with a seamless and intuitive banking experience. As part of the app's feature set, Mintyn wants to introduce a budgeting feature that enables users to track their expenses, set financial goals, and manage their budgets effectively. This case study focuses on the UX design process for the budgeting feature in both dark and light mode, ensuring a consistent and user-friendly experience across different visual settings
My Contributions
As a solo designer, I contributed to the implementation of the budgeting feature in the banking app by conducting user research, creating intuitive information architecture and user flows, and designing visually appealing interfaces. I also conducted usability test and iterated on the design based on user feedback. By collaborating with developers, I ensure the accurate implementation of the feature.
The process
To begin the design process, we conducted user research and analysis to understand Mintyn Bank customer needs and pain points regarding budgeting and financial management. In this case, we conducted user surveys and interviews to gather insights into users' preferences, behaviors, and expectations.

Based on the research findings, we created an information architecture that organizes the budgeting feature's components logically. We developed user flows that outline how users will interact with the feature, ensuring a smooth and efficient experience from start to finish.

We created low-fidelity wireframes and prototypes to visualize the layout and functionality of the budgeting feature. We started with the main screens, such as the screen for introducing users to the feature, add incime and expenses and the budget dashboard. We iterated on the designs based on usability testing and feedback from stakeholders.
