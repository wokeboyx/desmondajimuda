Optiverse by Optimus Bank

The problem

Optimus Bank has a limited customer base due to its few physical branches, which restricts its ability to acquire new customers. To address this issue, the bank intends to implement digital banking services to reach banked and underbanked customers and provide premium mobile banking and internet banking services to retain existing customers. However, the challenge is to successfully implement these services and attract new customers while maintaining the loyalty of the current customer base.

The solution

We designed and developed a mobile app and a responsive web app that caters to the needs of both new and existing customers of Optimus Bank. The app functions as a self-service platform for customers to open accounts, add or transfer funds, pay bills, request and maintain cards, and even apply for loans. This mobile app provides a convenient alternative to in-person banking and allows customers to carry out all banking transactions from their mobile devices.

The impact

This solution did not only provide convenience to customers but also helped Optimus Bank overcome the limitations of its physical branches and expand its reach to underbanked customers. Overall, our design solution was successful in addressing the problem at hand by increasing Optimus Bank's customer base by 200% in the first month of launch and by 500% in three months. We also succeeded in enhancing the banking experience for Optimus Bank's customers.

My contributions

Solo designer, responsible for the design of the Optimus Bank mobile and web app from wireframing to user testing and high-fidelity prototype. I worked closely with the product team to understand the user needs and goals, and I created wireframes and prototypes that reflected these needs. I also conducted user testing to ensure that the designs were easy to use and met the needs of the target audience.

The team

1 project manager, 1 product manager, 6 engineers and 1 designer.

Timeline

June 2022 - November 2022

01

Research

As the designer for Techbnb's website redesign, I conducted extensive research and worked closely with stakeholders and developers to create a beautiful and functional design that provided a seamless user experience. I used wireframes and prototypes, incorporating user feedback to create a website that was both accessible and optimized for search engines. The end result exceeded expectations and supported Techbnb's mission of creating a sense of belonging around the world.

02

Initial design

During the Webflow development phase of the Techbnb project, I used the approved design to build the site's structure, add custom animations and interactions, and implement key features like search functionality and payment processing. I prioritized responsiveness and performance, and kept the Techbnb team in the loop with regular updates and feedback incorporation. By the end of this phase, the site was ready for testing and launch, and I was excited to see the project come to fruition.

THE RESULTS

The Techbnb website redesign was a success, resulting in increased engagement and bookings. Our custom search and payment processing improved user experience, and the responsive design ensured optimal performance. The updated design reflected Techbnb's brand, creating a cohesive look across platforms. A thoughtful design process led to a successful product that met the client's goals and exceeded expectations.
