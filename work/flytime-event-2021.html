Flytime Events 2021

The problem

The COVID-19 pandemic has resulted in numerous restrictions on physical events and gatherings, leading to Flytime Music Festival being unable to organize events for an extended period. However, with the lifting of the lockdown in December 2021, the organizers are planning a significant event. In order to ensure the safety of attendees, they need a ticketing feature that allows for contactless purchasing of event tickets

The solution

To solve the problem of contactless ticketing, I designed a feature in the Clane mobile app and website for Flytime Music Festival. The feature allows users to find the Flytime event they want to attend and purchase tickets with ease. The feature also includes restrictions to ensure the safety of attendees during the pandemic.Users can also gift purchased tickets to friends or family through the app. They can also reassign tickets to another user if they are unable to attend the event. Additionally, users can request tickets from other ClanePay users.

The impact

The Clane mobile app and website for Flytime Music Festival offers a secure and touch-free method for users to buy, exchange, and present tickets using its ticketing feature. It is designed to be user-friendly and guarantees the safety of participants during the pandemic. As a result, both business partners achieved their objectives, with all Flytime events in December 2021 selling out. Clane also experienced a 50% rise in monthly signups and active users, fulfilling the business goal of increasing user acquisition through the Flytime event partnership.

My contributions

Responsible for in-depth research, and end-to-end design of the event ticket sales experiences across mobile and desktop device.

The team

1 product manager, 3 engineers and 1 designer.

Timeline

November 2021 - December 2021

01

Research

As the designer for Techbnb's website redesign, I conducted extensive research and worked closely with stakeholders and developers to create a beautiful and functional design that provided a seamless user experience. I used wireframes and prototypes, incorporating user feedback to create a website that was both accessible and optimized for search engines. The end result exceeded expectations and supported Techbnb's mission of creating a sense of belonging around the world.

02

Initial design

During the Webflow development phase of the Techbnb project, I used the approved design to build the site's structure, add custom animations and interactions, and implement key features like search functionality and payment processing. I prioritized responsiveness and performance, and kept the Techbnb team in the loop with regular updates and feedback incorporation. By the end of this phase, the site was ready for testing and launch, and I was excited to see the project come to fruition.

THE RESULTS

The Techbnb website redesign was a success, resulting in increased engagement and bookings. Our custom search and payment processing improved user experience, and the responsive design ensured optimal performance. The updated design reflected Techbnb's brand, creating a cohesive look across platforms. A thoughtful design process led to a successful product that met the client's goals and exceeded expectations.
