Optiverse by Optimus Bank

DESIGN FOR MOBILE & DESKTOP
Overview

Optimus Bank is a financial institution that offers a wide range of financial services, including personal banking, business banking, and lending solutions. They provide their customers with access to both online and mobile banking, as well as a network of ATMs. In mid-2022, Optimus Bank partnered with Clane Labs to create a mobile banking app and responsive web app (internet banking) that catered to their existing customers, onboarded new customers, and served the underbanked population, marking their initial entry into the world of digital banking.

My role

Solo designer, responsible for the design of mobile banking app and responsive web app.

THe team

1 designer, 1 project manager, 1 product manager 5+ engineers.

Timeline

June 2022 - November 2022

the problem

Optimus Bank has a limited customer base due to its few physical branches, which restricts its ability to acquire new customers. To address this issue, the bank intends to implement digital banking services to reach banked and underbanked customers and provide premium mobile banking and internet banking services to retain existing customers. However, the challenge is to successfully implement these services and attract new customers while maintaining the loyalty of the current customer base.

Design solution

Our solution involved developing a mobile app and a responsive web app that caters to the needs of both new and existing customers of Optimus Bank. The app functions as a self-service platform for customers to open accounts, add or transfer funds, pay bills, request and maintain cards, and even apply for loans. This mobile app provides a convenient alternative to in-person banking and allows customers to carry out all banking transactions from their mobile devices.

Conclusion

Our solution not only provided convenience to customers but also helped Optimus Bank overcome the limitations of its physical branches and expand its reach to underbanked customers. Overall, our design solution was successful in addressing the problem at hand by increasing Optimus Bank's customer base by 200% in the first month of launch and by 500% in three months. We also succeeded in enhancing the banking experience for Optimus Bank's customers.
