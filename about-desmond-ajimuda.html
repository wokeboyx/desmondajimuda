DESMOND AJIMUDA

I'm a product designer and manager, who is passionate about helping companies create products that make a difference in people's lives. I am currently working as a product designer & design system manager at Hydrogen. Formerly, I helped build products at Clane, Optimus Bank, Mintyn Digital Bank, Sterling Bank & Nugi Technologies.
‍
I have been professionally designing since I was 18 years old as a graphic designer and creative director for my T-shirt brand (AVERTI COUTURE). I became a product designer in 2019, Since then I’ve discovered my true passion for creating digital products that solve real problems.
‍
My passion lay at the intersection of design and growth. I love being able to create beautiful and functional products that solved real problems, while also using data and analytics to drive their success.

Aside work I am a music enthusiast, in my spare time I create music playlist on Spotify.
